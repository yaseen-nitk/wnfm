Welcome to Wash n Fold & Market!

Our story is one of dreams, perseverance, and a commitment to making everyday tasks easier for you.

Our Journey

Our founder's journey began with over sixteen years of experience as an accounting teacher. Teaching was more than just a job; it was a passion. He also ran Misbah School, a tuition center, every evening, dedicating his time to educating students.

In November 2000, he moved to the United States with dreams of new opportunities. Starting over wasn't easy. He worked in fast food at KFC, Taco Bell, and Whataburger, gaining valuable insights into the fast-paced American food industry. His entrepreneurial spirit led him to join Spoon Rocket, a California startup, which aimed to deliver food within ten minutes. Though the company eventually shut down, the experience was invaluable, teaching him about the importance of speed and efficiency in service.

Overcoming Challenges

Life in the U.S. presented its own set of challenges, from job market resistance to personal and financial hardships, including a period of bankruptcy in 2018. Despite these setbacks, our founder’s spiritual roots, influenced by Sufi teachings and his father, Abdul Aziz Misbah, kept him grounded. Cooking became a source of solace and connection, a way to find peace and community.

Our Vision

Wash and Fold Market was born from these experiences and challenges. It’s not just another laundry service or convenience store app; it’s a platform designed to bring ease and comfort to your daily routine. With Wash and Fold Market, you can schedule laundry pickups and deliveries, track your laundry in real-time, and access a wide range of essential items like snacks, drinks, toiletries, and more—all from the convenience of your phone.

We believe in providing reliable and high-quality services that make a difference in your day. Our goal is to offer more than just convenience; we aim to create moments of relief and comfort with every service we provide.

Dry Cleaning

For those special garments that require extra care, our dry cleaning service is just what you need. We use the best techniques and solutions to clean your delicate fabrics, ensuring they look as good as new. Whether it’s your favorite suit, dress, or any other item, we handle it with the utmost care and professionalism.

Steam Press

Give your clothes a crisp, polished look with our steam press service. Perfect for business attire, formal wear, and everyday clothes, our steam press ensures your garments are wrinkle-free and impeccably pressed. Schedule our service to keep your wardrobe looking sharp and professional.
